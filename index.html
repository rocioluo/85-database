Background

85 movement is an important period in Chinese contemporary art. Showing the impact of western culture following China's opening and reform (Gao ,2008), numerous unofficial art groups appeared spontaneously from 1984 to 1986, leading 85 art new wave to this exuberant period in the history (Wu, 2010). As the participator and artist, Professor Gao experienced that movement, and fortunately, he holds a lot of first-hand information including artists' letters, conference letter, and photos of exhibitions. Most of them are organized in his book the 85 movement, an anthology of historical sources. I appreciate a lot that he admits me to use his work as row data to do my research.
